Welcome to GiveMeCredit!

This app is under construction. The goal is to make alternative currencies more accessible. It is open-source and not-for-profit. If you are a developer and would like to help out, I would really appreciate it. You can contact me at:- glen_simister@yahoo.co.uk

If you want someone to give you credit, simply paste your unique id next to something you would like credit for. Your link will look something like this :- http://gmc@MYUNIQUEIDXYZ123.com. The browser plugin will automatically convert it to a widget like so:-
